Deep Software Engineering Lead

The Deep Software Engineering Lead leads a team of software engineers to create high quality machine learning enterprise software systems. Not only will you code but you will manage others who code. As a Lead you will manage project goals, take charge of large scale software architecture, and enforce strong software engineering processes.

Responsibilities
  • Ensure the highest standard of product quality and reliability.
  • Drive technical projects and provide leadership.
  • Take responsibility for the overall planning, execution and success of complex technical projects.
  • Contribute technically to projects.
  • Work with external parties to ensure interoperability.
  • Take charge of task planning and code reviews.
